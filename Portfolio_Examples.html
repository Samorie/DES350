Good Portfolio Examples

By Sammy Lien | DES 350

Portfolio 1 - https://www.wokine.com

  • This portfolio works because the hierachy is very prominent and the design is very effective yet minimal and simplistic.
  • The site has nice contrast and good balance of colors.
  • The overall composition of the website makes it very easy to navigate.

    • Portfolio 2 - http://seanhalpin.io/

  • I really like the use of the pastel color schemes.
  • The use of cartoons and doodles as his imagery is really playful. Overall, the portfolio is very simple and creative which makes it fun to navigate the site.
  • I really like how in his 'works' tab, the background colors compliment to the color palette of his web designs.

    • Portfolio 3 - https://bepatrickdavid.com/

  • The overall layout of the website is very interesting. It's one page that you simply scroll throughout.
  • Good balance of colors and contrast with nice composition and type.
  • All of his works are displayed at the bottom rather than just a link to a different page.
